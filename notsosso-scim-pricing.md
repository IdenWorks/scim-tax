## Notes & Caveats

- **Coda, Crowdin, DeepL, Dropbox, DMARC Report** — pricing pages are JS-rendered; data may be incomplete. Manual verification recommended.
- **Deel** — blocked by Cloudflare bot protection. SCIM provisioning is known to exist in Deel HR but was not confirmable from the provided URL.
- **Greenhouse** — uses the term "Automated user management" rather than SCIM explicitly. All plans are contact-sales with no public pricing.
- **BlueJeans** — shut down by Verizon, March 2024.
- **InVision Freehand** — permanently redirects to Miro; discontinued.
- **Hotjar** — absorbed into Contentsquare; old pricing URL redirects.
- **Hub Planner** — redirects to Milient Software.
- **RingCentral** — SCIM 2.0 available across all paid plans, but requires pre-purchasing licenses before SCIM can provision new users.
- **ClickTime** — unusual: SCIM offered as a separate add-on across all plan tiers (not locked to top tier), but requires a custom quote.
- **Airtable** — split SCIM: user provisioning at Business tier, group management requires Enterprise Scale. A meaningful and notable pricing split.
