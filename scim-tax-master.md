# SCIM Tax — Master Research File

**Purpose:** Comprehensive competitive intelligence for Iden's SCIM Tax narrative  
**Date compiled:** 2026-04-02  
**Sources:** notsosso.com, sso.tax, top 250 SaaS cross-reference (Okta BAW, Zylo, Ramp Velocity, Productiv), Okta Integration Network, Rippling App Shop  
**Total vendors surveyed:** ~285  

---

## The Core Finding

**SCIM — the protocol that automates user provisioning and deprovisioning — is systematically gated behind Enterprise tiers across the SaaS universe.**

Of ~285 vendors surveyed:
- ~160 offer SCIM in some form
- ~130 of those gate it behind Enterprise / Contact Sales pricing
- ~20 have a published price for the SCIM tier
- ~10 offer SCIM below Enterprise tier
- ~8 offer SCIM on all plans with no paywall
- ~40 have no SCIM support at any price
- ~85 do not mention SCIM at all on their pricing page

The result: organizations that want automated identity governance — not just SSO, but actual lifecycle management — pay a systematic premium across their entire SaaS stack. This is the SCIM Tax.

## Research Methodology & Caveats

### Sources
- **notsosso.com** — community-maintained list of vendors with SSO tax; all pricing pages visited directly
- **sso.tax** — separate community list; new-only vendors not duplicated from notsosso.com
- **Top SaaS cross-reference** — Okta Businesses at Work report, Zylo SaaS Management Index, Ramp Velocity spend data, Productiv usage data; ~55 gap vendors added
- **Final gap batch** — 16 additional enterprise/security/finance vendors added after Okta Integration Network and Rippling App Shop review

### Known Data Gaps and Limitations
- **JS-rendered pricing pages** (Coda, Crowdin, DeepL, Dropbox, DMARC Report) — WebFetch returned empty or unusable content. These entries are flagged; manual verification recommended.
- **Cloudflare-blocked pages** — Deel's help center returned 403. SCIM is believed to exist in Deel HR but was not confirmable.
- **Playvox** — pricing page blocked; data from third-party sources only. Manual verification needed.
- **GatherContent** — acquired by Bynder; pricing no longer public.
- **Fully opaque vendors** — Gong, Gainsight, ServiceNow, Workday, etc. require a sales call before SCIM existence or pricing is disclosed. Estimates noted as (~est.) are based on analyst reports and third-party data.
- **Yodeck** — price increase announced effective April 2026; verify current rates.
- **Pricing page URLs** — URLs were compiled from direct research and known patterns. A small number of less common vendors may have changed URL structure; verify if any link returns a 404.

### Definitional Notes
- **SCIM vs. JIT**: SCIM (RFC 7644) supports create, update, and **deprovision**. JIT (Just-in-Time via SAML) only creates a user on first login and does not handle deprovisioning. JIT entries are noted as partial/not SCIM.
- **Directory Sync**: Used as a marketing synonym for SCIM by some vendors (Deepnote, Mattermost, Ashby/WorkOS). Treated as equivalent where the underlying protocol is confirmed SCIM 2.0.
- **IdP-side provisioning**: Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Okta, JumpCloud, Rippling — these push SCIM outbound to other apps. They are not paying a SCIM tax; they are the infrastructure that makes SCIM possible for everything else.
- **Marketplace app provisioning**: Freshdesk and Freshservice deliver SCIM through a free marketplace app, but the app is only accessible on Pro+ tiers. The SCIM gate is the plan, not the app cost.
- **Airtable split**: User provisioning available at Business tier. Group management requires Enterprise Scale. A meaningful and noteworthy pricing split.
- **RingCentral**: SCIM 2.0 available across all paid plans, but requires pre-purchasing licenses before SCIM can provision new users — a hidden operational constraint.
