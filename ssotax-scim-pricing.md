### Notable Anomalies

| Anomaly | Vendor | Detail |
|---|---|---|
| **Explicit $500/mo SCIM add-on** | Temporal Cloud | SCIM is a line-item add-on at $500/mo on Business — the most transparent SCIM tax in the dataset |
| **SCIM explicitly unsupported** | Sauce Labs | Listed as "Unsupported Features" in their SSO setup docs |
| **SCIM needs a separate product** | OpsGenie | SCIM requires purchasing Atlassian Guard Standard separately; not bundled in any OpsGenie plan |
| **Lowest SCIM price point** | BitWarden | $4/user/mo Teams — 18x cheaper than Slack's $15/user Business+; included at Teams, not Enterprise-only |
| **SCIM gated by IdP choice** | Adobe Creative Cloud | SCIM works only with Azure AD or Google Workspace — Okta excluded despite Adobe being in Okta's network |
| **Most expensive SCIM jump** | JFrog | Pro ($150/mo) → Enterprise X ($950/mo) — 6.3x jump for SCIM access |
| **Only vendor pricing SCIM visibly on-page (besides Wedo/Yodeck)** | Box, Slack, LinearB, Temporal | Have published prices for SCIM-tier plans; most others say "Contact Sales" |

## Notes & Caveats

- **Overlapping vendors:** Many sso.tax vendors (Airtable, Asana, Atlassian, Calendly, Figma, GitHub, etc.) are already documented in `notsosso-scim-pricing.md`. This file covers new/unique vendors only.
- **Playvox** — pricing page blocked; data from third-party sources only. Manual verification needed.
- **GatherContent** — acquired by Bynder; pricing no longer public. Flagged as unable to confirm.
- **Hatz.ai** — MSP-distributed; no public pricing. No SCIM evidence found.
- **Expensify** — SCIM token field exists in Okta SSO setup flow, but is not marketed as a plan feature and requires manual request via concierge.
- **VictorOps / Splunk On-Call** — SCIM documented in Okta setup guides, but not self-service; requires engaging Splunk Support regardless of plan.
- **Mattermost** — AD/LDAP group sync and auto-provisioning on Professional tier, but "SCIM" is not used by name in their marketing materials.
- **Wedo** — all pricing displayed in CHF (Swiss Francs).
- **Yodeck** — price increase announced effective April 2026; verify current rates.
